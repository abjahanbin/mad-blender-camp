Drawing Simulation

Drawing / 2D

Daily warm-up with a physics focus. Draw objects moving under real forces — the arc of a thrown ball, the cascade of falling cloth, the ripple of a collision, the bounce of something elastic. Let gravity and momentum guide the line. Simulate with your hand before the computer does it.

Physics Simulation

Modeling / 3D

Blender's physics engine can simulate rigid bodies, cloth, soft bodies, fluid, smoke, and destruction. Set up a simulation, bake it, and render a sequence where the computer calculates every collision, fold, and splash. Real physical forces, virtual matter — animation that you couldn't keyframe by hand.

Mixing & Editing

Sound Design

Bring all your recorded and designed audio together. Balance levels so nothing is too loud or buried, shape frequencies with EQ, add reverb and spatial width to give sounds depth and placement. Build a final audio timeline that supports your animation — tight enough to feel intentional, spacious enough to breathe.
