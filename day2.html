Drawing Exercises

Drawing / 2D

Daily warm-up sketching to open the session. Today's focus: drawing 3D objects from observation — cups, boxes, bottles, chairs. Learn to capture volume and depth on a flat page before building it in Blender.

Modeling with Primitive Objects

Modeling / 3D

Build complex shapes from simple ones. Every 3D model ever made — from a film character to a video game environment — started with a cube, sphere, or cylinder. Learn to combine, cut, stretch, and reshape primitives to make anything you can imagine.

Shape People: 3D Character Design

Modeling / 3D

Design and model a simple character figure using only basic shapes — no fine detail, no rigging, just pure geometry and personality. You'll be surprised how much expression you can get out of a few spheres and boxes arranged just right.
