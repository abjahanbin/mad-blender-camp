Drawing Exercises

Drawing / 2D

Daily warm-up sketching. Today's focus: character gesture and posture. Draw figures communicating emotion through body language alone — no faces, just poses. Loose and fast. A slumped shoulder says something. So does a raised fist.

Working with Mixamo for Character Animation

Animation

Mixamo is a free tool from Adobe that auto-rigs 3D characters and applies motion capture animation in minutes. Upload a character mesh, choose from hundreds of pre-built animations — walk cycles, dances, combat moves — and bring it back into Blender ready to render. Full character animation without building a rig from scratch.

Recording Basics

Sound Design

Learn to capture your own audio — voice, ambient sound, and foley effects. We'll cover microphone placement and technique, gain staging so recordings aren't too quiet or distorted, and basic monitoring. Record source material from the room around you that you can layer, manipulate, and use in later sessions.
