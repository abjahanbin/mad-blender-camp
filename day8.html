Drawing Exercises

Drawing / 2D

Daily warm-up sketching. Today's focus: rhythm and beat on the page. Translate a piece of music into a sequence of marks — one frame per beat, one drawing per section. Experiment with how tempo becomes visual pacing.

Audio Reactive Animation

Animation

Drive your 3D animation directly with audio data. Blender's driver system lets you link sound frequencies to object properties — a bass hit makes something jump, a high frequency controls rotation speed, amplitude controls scale. Your scene doesn't just play alongside the music — it listens and responds.

Ambience, Music & Emotional Beats

Sound Design

Sound design is emotional design. Learn how ambient layers, musical key and mode, tempo, and dynamic range shape the feeling of a scene. A room that's silent is tense. The same room with a low drone is ominous. With gentle strings it's melancholic. We'll compose a short atmospheric piece that gives a 3D world its emotional texture.
