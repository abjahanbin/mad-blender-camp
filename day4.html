Story & Loops

Drawing Exercises

Drawing / 2D

Daily warm-up sketching. Today's focus: storyboard panels — action, reaction, transition. Draw quick thumbnail sequences that show a moment before, during, and after an event.

Storyboarding for Storytelling

Drawing / 2D

Plan your animation before you make it. Storyboarding is the blueprint — it's how every film, short, and commercial starts. Learn to thumbnail shots, think in cuts, choose camera angles, and communicate a sequence of events without a single frame of footage.

Loop Lab: GIFs in Blender

Animation

Create a seamlessly looping animation and export it as a GIF. Loops are satisfying to make and perfect for sharing. We'll work on timing, easing, and that satisfying moment when the end snaps cleanly back to the beginning.
