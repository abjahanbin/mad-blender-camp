Sculpt & World Build

Drawing Exercises

Drawing / 2D

Daily warm-up sketching. Today's focus: environment sketching and world-building concepts. Draw a place — real or imagined — and think about what makes it feel like somewhere.

3D Sculpting in Blender

Modeling / 3D

Move beyond box modeling into organic form. Blender's sculpt mode lets you push, pull, pinch, and smooth geometry like digital clay. No more boxy topology — sculpt faces, creatures, terrain, or whatever shape is in your head.

Photogrammetry and Alternative Modeling

Modeling / 3D

You don't always have to build a model from scratch. Photogrammetry lets you photograph a real-world object from multiple angles and turn it into a 3D mesh. We'll scan objects from the room around us and pull them into Blender.

World Building: Ambient Animation

Animation

Build a complete atmospheric 3D scene — lighting, materials, subtle motion, and mood. This is the capstone of Week 1: bring together everything you've learned and make something that feels like a world. Subtle environmental animation (drifting particles, swaying objects, light shifts) transforms a static render into something alive.
